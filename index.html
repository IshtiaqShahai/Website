About the Lab

Who Built This Lab.

I started Ventax Ai Lab in 2026. The idea was simple. Most AI tools cannot help you with the kind of paperwork Pakistani offices actually run on. So I started building tools that can.

My name is Shah. I am an electrical engineer with a Master's degree. Before this lab I worked for ten years inside the government. My last role was Staff Officer to the Secretary of Energy in Khyber Pakhtunkhwa.

That experience matters. I have drafted Working Papers myself. I have prepared ECNEC summaries. I have walked files through approval. The software I build today carries all of that inside it.

The lab now ships retrieval pipelines. Multi-agent systems. Vision tools that read scanned forms. Full web applications. Each one is built for a domain I know from the inside.
